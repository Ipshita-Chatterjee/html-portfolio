The Best Movies According to Ipshita

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Sanam Teri Kasam

This is my romance movie. It brings me to tears every single time that I watch it, both in a good and bad way.

3 Idiots

Really inspiring movie which showcases us why its so important to have true friends and enjoy life at every single moment.

Phir Hera Pheri

Super funny movie and you can always have a nice laugh while watching it with your family.
